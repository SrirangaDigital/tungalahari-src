dāśarathe māṁ pālaya

dāśarathe māṁ pālaya dāritadaśavadana ।
dīnajanāvanadīkṣa duritāpahavīkṣa ॥

kausalyāpriyatanaya kauśikamukhavinuta ।
daṇḍitadaityavrāta khaṇḍitaharacāpa ॥ 1 ॥

kharadūṣaṇamukharakṣo vidalananipuṇaśara ।
kharakiraṇānvayasāgara rākāśiśirakara ॥ 2 ॥

bhavabhayanāśanacatura bhajatāmabhayakara ।
bharatādyanujasameta bhāratītīrthanuta ॥ 3 ॥
