॥ śrīdattātreyastavaḥ ॥

dattātreyapadābjaṁ
vittādhīśādidevavaryeḍyam ।
hṛttāpahāracaturaṁ
cittāmalabhāvasiddhaye naumi ॥ 1 ॥

anasūyāsutamīḍe
vanasañcāraprasaktacetaskam ।
kanakapradānanirataṁ
vanamālābhūṣitagrīvam ॥ 2 ॥

avimuktasnānarataṁ
kaॊlhāpuramadhyamantrajapaśīlam ।
sahyādriśayanalolaṁ
mādhūkarabhaikṣyabhojanaṁ vande ॥ 3 ॥

āśāmbaraṁ prapadye
pāśāpahamāśu pādanamrāṇām ।
keśāvadhūtasūryaṁ
kāśāgracalanmanonirodhāya ॥ 4 ॥

audumbaratarumūle
kṛtavāsaṁ kṛṣṇabhaktidānaratam ।
audumbarādidivya-
kṣetrāvāsaṁ namāmi yogīśam ॥ 5 ॥

śaradindutulyavadanaṁ
kararājadbodhamudramatisulabham ।
taraṇāya bhavapayodheḥ
śaraṇaṁ yāsyāmi dattayogīndram ॥ 6 ॥

kṛtavīryaputradeśika-
mindupratimānavadanakañjātam ।
bimbādharamanasūyā-
mukhapadmārkaṁ namāmi gurudattam ॥ 7 ॥

kṛttāghabṛnda namatāṁ
cittāmbujamadhyasaṁstha bhaktānām ।
dattātreya kṛpābdhe
yattākṣārādhyacaraṇa māṁ pāhi ॥ 8 ॥

kravyādavairirūpo
navyāmbudasannikāśadeharuciḥ ।
bhavyāya bhavatu yogī
nirvyājadayāsudhāmbudhiḥ ko'pi ॥ 9 ॥

gativijitamattanāgaṁ
matidānadhurandharaṁ praṇamrebhyaḥ ।
yatitatipūjitapādaṁ
patitāvanadīkṣamenamākalaye ॥ 10 ॥

gorakṣakamukhayogibhi-
rādarataḥ sevyamānapadapadmam ।
gobrāhmaṇahitakāraṇa-
matreḥ sutamātanomi hṛtpadme ॥ 11 ॥

ghuṭikāñjanamukhasiddhī-
rghaṭikātaḥ prāpnuvanti yadbhaktāḥ ।
sphaṭikākṣamālikāḍhyaṁ
taṭidābhaṁ naumi taṁ dattam ॥ 12 ॥

dinanāthatulyavasanaṁ
diṣṭyaikaprāpyacaraṇapāthojam ।
divi bhuvi pātāle ca
pracarantaṁ naumi yoginaṁ kañcit ॥ 13 ॥

bālonmattasadṛkṣaṁ
kālotpannāśanena santuṣṭam ।
mālollasadgalataṭaṁ
kālocchityai namāmi dattamunim ॥ 14 ॥

bharatāgrajabhaktāryaṁ
bhadrāṇi diśantamāśu namrebhyaḥ ।
bhavabhītibhañjanacaṇaṁ
dattātreyaṁ namāmi modena ॥ 15 ॥

bhavanīkṛtānatahṛdaṁ
bhaktājñānebhadalanapañcāsyam ।
bhasitāvaliptagātraṁ
bhavyakaraṁ naumi dattamunivaryam ॥ 16 ॥

yasyāṣṭavidhā mantrāḥ
śīghraṁ siddhipradāyakāstiṣye ।
tasyāṅghripaṅkajamahaṁ
dattātreyasya naumi yogīśaḥ ॥ 17 ॥

yāgādikarmalabhyaṁ
bhogāliṁ namrapaṅktaye dadatam ।
yogādhirūḍhamaniśaṁ
nāgādhipaśāyinaṁ namasyāmi ॥ 18 ॥

ravadhūtahaṁsagarvaṁ
navanavakavitāpradānadhaureyam ।
vidhihariśivasvarūpaṁ
pravaṇaṁ vande natāvane kañcit ॥ 19 ॥

vairāgyadānanirataṁ
tīrātikrāntavaibhavopetam ।
kārābandhavimocaka-
mairāvatayāyisevitaṁ naumi ॥ 20 ॥

śaraṇāgataparirakṣaṇa-
nirataṁ taruṇaṁ tamālasamadeham ।
sarasijasadṛkṣanayanaṁ
śaraṇaṁ yāsyāmi dattamuninātham ॥ 21 ॥

kathitāgatārthasāraṁ
mathitākhiladuḥkhakāraṇāvidyam ।
pathi parame sthitamaniśaṁ
śithilitapāpaughamāśraye dattam ॥ 22 ॥

śīrṣatrayeṇa sahitaṁ
śīrṣaṁ vedatrayasya natalokān ।
sambodhayantamaniśaṁ
bhāvayatu svāntamādarādyaminam ॥ 23 ॥

smṛtimātratuṣṭahṛdayaṁ
matidaṁ tarasāṅghrinamralokāya ।
śitikaṇṭhamitramīḍe
kṛtināṁ pratyakṣamanvahaṁ dattam ॥ 24 ॥

gorakṣādyairmukhyasuśiṣyaiḥ parivītaṁ
goviprāṇāṁ poṣaṇasaktaṁ karuṇābdhim ।
golakṣmīśāmbujabhavagirijāsakharūpaṁ
dattātreyaśrīpadapadmaṁ praṇato'smi ॥ 25 ॥
