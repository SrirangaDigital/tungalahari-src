॥ bhavabandhamuktyaṣṭakam ॥

ekaṁ dvitīyarahitaṁ sadabādhitaṁ ca
saccitsvarūpamiti yacchrutiśīrṣapūgaiḥ ।
jegīyate sakala lokavivartabhūtaṁ
tadbhāvayāmi satataṁ bhavabandhamuktyai ॥ 1 ॥

annāsumānasamukhaprakṛtīñjagāda
kośāñchrutiryadavabodhakṛte'tra pañca ।
sarvāntaraṁ bhṛgumunipravareṇa dṛṣṭaṁ
tadbhāvayāmi satataṁ bhavabandhamuktyai ॥ 2 ॥

ānāśakena tapasā bahudakṣiṇena
yajñena dānanicayācchrutipāṭhataśca ।
icchanti vettumiha yaddharaṇīsurāgryā-
stadbhāvayāmi satataṁ bhavabandhamuktyai ॥ 3 ॥

kaścidvipaścidiha saṁsṛtisaukhyavāñchāṁ
santyajya sadgurumupetya kṛpāpayobdhim ।
vijñāya tadvacanataḥ khalu modate yat
tadbhāvayāmi satataṁ bhavabandhamuktyai ॥ 4 ॥

prāṇānniyamya tu mano hṛdayāravinde
bhrūgahvare śirasi vā praṇidhāya samyak ।
dhyāyanti yatparagurorvacanānusārāt
tadbhāvayāmi satataṁ bhavabandhamuktyai ॥ 5 ॥

yajjāgradādisamaye dhṛtaviśvamukhya-
nāmātanoti bahirantaravastusevām ।
suptāvabodhasahitasya sukhasya bhoktṛ
tadbhāvayāmi satataṁ bhavabandhamuktyai ॥ 6 ॥

vāgādayaḥ svaviṣayeṣu caranti yena
sañcoditāḥ prabhuvareṇa yathā subhṛtyāḥ ।
tatsarvakāryakaraṇavyavahārasākṣī
sañcintayāmi satataṁ bhavabandhamuktyai ॥ 7 ॥

saṁnyasya karmanicayaṁ ca tadaṅgabhūtaṁ
sūtraṁ śikhāṁ ca punarapyabalādirāgam ।
bodhāya yasya yatate pariśuddhacitta-
stadbhāvayāmi satataṁ bhavabandhamuktyai ॥ 8 ॥

padyāṣṭakaṁ paṭhati yo'rthavibodhapūrvaṁ
sañcintayannanudinaṁ pratipādyavastu ।
bhaktyā yutaḥ kaluṣadūranijāntaraṅga-
stasyācirāddhi bhavitā bhavabandhamuktiḥ ॥ 9 ॥
