॥ śrīpadmanābhastotram ॥

padmanābha karuṇāmṛtarāśe
sadmahetulavaśūnyakṛpāyāḥ ।
chadmahāritamahābalisampa-
dvidma eva tava vīryamanantam ॥ 1 ॥

nābhyāḥ sarastvena hi varṇanaṁ tu
satyaṁ tadā syātkila padmanābha ।
yadā tvadīyāṁ viṣayīkaroti
nābhiṁ na cedyāti kathaṁ pramātvam ॥ 2 ॥

nābhīpadme putraṁ
vakṣasi kāntāṁ ca sandadhattāntaḥ ।
svapiṣi sadā kiṁ viṣṇo
namrāyāsmai drutaṁ brūhi ॥ 3 ॥

pāpābdhau vaḍavānalasya hi śikhā jāḍyāndhakāre ravi-
rbhāgyābdhau śaradindupaṅktiramalā tvannāmasaṅkīrtanam ।
bhaktānāṁ paripoṣaṇe ca jananī svātmāvabodhe pitā
kastasmādvimukho bhavedbhuvi naro no cenmahāpāpabhāk ॥ 4 ॥

kṣīrāmbhonidhirājamānaphaṇirāṇmadhye śayānā sadā
lakṣmīpāṇipayojamarditapadā kāruṇyavārāṁnidhiḥ ।
kurvāṇā madhunā ca kaiṭabhayujā yuddhaṁ ca dorbhyāṁ muhu-
rmudrā kācana muktidānakuśalā nidrāti jāgarti vā ॥ 5 ॥

padmanābha tava pādasarojā-
saktacittamavanīpatimenam ।
pāhi sāśritasabandhusavidva-
llokamādarabhareṇa sarāṣṭram ॥ 6 ॥

sāmodaṁ pradiśantu vañcikulabhūmūlarkṣarājāya vā-
gdevīcandrakalādharebhavadanāḥ kāruṇyavārāśayaḥ ।
dīrghāyurdṛḍhagātratāmalayaśāṁsyācandratigmāṁśubhaṁ
dharmādhvanyadhikāṁ pravṛttimacalāmacchinnasatsantatim ॥ 7 ॥

pāyāsurjagadambikenduvilasacchīrṣebhavaktrāḥ sadā
rājñau keraladeśabhāgyalatike kāruṇyayugbhirmudā ।
dṛgbhiḥ pādavinamrasaukhyavitateḥ sampādikābhirjavā-
ddattvā santatimāśu sadguṇayutāmārogyadīrghāyuṣī ॥ 8 ॥

mocitaḥ kiṁ gajādhīśaḥ
padmanābhena satvaram ।
dṛḍhabhāvanayā tasya
padmanābho'bhavadgajeṭ ॥ 9 ॥

śrībhūmipāṇisarasīruhasevitāṅghre
śrīvatsakaustubhadhara praṇatārtihārin ।
śrīkaṇṭhamitra dinanāthasudhāṁśunetra
śrīpadmanābha mama dehi padāvalambam ॥ 10 ॥

nāgendrabhogaśayana praṇatālivāñchā-
sampūraṇāmarataro vidhiyoninābhe ।
nākādhipādiparipūjitapādapadma
śrīpadmanābha mama dehi padāvalambam ॥ 11 ॥

kamalākarapadmapūjitaṁ
karuṇādugdhapayodhimadhyagam ।
bhujagādhipabhogaśāyinaṁ
kalaye kañcana devasattamam ॥ 12 ॥
