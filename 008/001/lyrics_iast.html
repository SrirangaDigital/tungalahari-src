॥ śrīkṛṣṇaṣaṭkam ॥

bhagavatpadakarapaṅkaja-
pūjitapadapadma śaṅkhacakradhara ।
yadunandana natanandana
pāhi sadā māṁ kṛpāpayorāśe ॥ 1 ॥

navanītādapyadhikaṁ
mṛdu mama hṛdayaṁ praṇamrarakṣāyām ।
iti bodhayituṁ dhatse
navanītaṁ kiṁ kare kṛṣṇa ॥ 2 ॥

gogopamuktidaṁ tvāṁ
gobhāraharaṁ kṛpāpayorāśim ।
gopījanārcitapadaṁ
goprāptyai naumi devakīsūnum ॥ 3 ॥

govardhanamandaradhara-
dharaṇādgarvāyase vṛthā śaure ।
yadyasti śaktiradhunā
dhara māṁ dharaṇīramārcitāṅghriyuga ॥ 4 ॥

alpabalakaiṭabhādīn
hatvā garvaṁ vṛthā kuruṣe ।
mohābhidhānamasuraṁ
jahi śīghraṁ śaktirasi yadi kṛṣṇa ॥ 5 ॥

śaṅkaraguruvarakaravara-
kamalārcitāṅghriyuga kṛṣṇa ।
kiṅkaravarajanabṛndaṁ
toṣaya toyāliśayana kamaleśa ॥ 6 ॥
