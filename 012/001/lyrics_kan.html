ಶ್ರೀಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಸ್ತವ

ವರಮಭಯಮುದಾರಂ ಪುಸ್ತಕಂ ಚಾಕ್ಷಹಾರಂ
ಮಣಿವಲಯಮನೋಜ್ಞೈಃ ಪಾಣಿಪದ್ಮೈರ್ದಧಾನಾ ।
ಸಿತವಸನಲಲಾಮಾ ಕುನ್ದಮುಕ್ತಾಭಿರಾಮಾ
ವಸತು ಶಶಿನಿಭಾಸ್ಯಾ ವಾಚಿ ವಾಗ್ದೇವತಾ ನಃ ॥

ಶ್ರೀ ಸಚ್ಚಿದಾನನ್ದಶಿವಾಭಿನವ-
ನೃಸಿಂಹಭಾರತ್ಯಭಿಧಾನ್ಯತೀನ್ದ್ರಾನ್ ।
ವಿದ್ಯಾನಿಧೀನ್ ಮನ್ತ್ರನಿಧೀನ್ ಸದಾತ್ಮನಿಷ್ಠಾನ್
ಭಜೇ ಮಾನವಶಮ್ಭುರೂಪಾನ್ ॥

ಭಾರತೀಕರುಣಾಪಾತ್ರಂ ಭಾರತೀಪದಭೂಷಣಂ ।
ಭಾರತೀಪದಮಾರೂಢಂ ಭಾರತೀತೀರ್ಥಮಾಶ್ರಯೇ ॥

ಪುರತೋ ಭವತು ಕೃತಾಬ್ಧಿಃ
ಪುರವೈರಿನಿಷ್ಠಮಾನಸಃ ಸೋಽಯಂ ।
ಪರಮಶಿವೇನ್ದ್ರಕರಾಮ್ಬುಜಸಞ್ಜಾತೋ ಯಃ
ಸದಾಶಿವೇನ್ದ್ರೋ ಮೇ ॥

ಪರತತ್ತ್ವಲೀನಮನಸೇ
ಪ್ರಣಮದ್ಭವಬನ್ಧಮೋಚನಾಯಾಶು ।
ಪ್ರಕಟಿತಪರತತ್ತ್ವಾಯ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೧ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra (Sadasiva
Brahmendral), whose mind is
immersed in the Supreme Truth, who
reveals the ultimate reality and
immediately liberates one from the
shackles of transmigratory existence
to those who take refuge in Him

ಪರಮಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಕರಾಮ್ಬುಜ-
ಸಂಭೂತಾಯ ಪ್ರಣಮ್ರವರದಾಯ । ಪದಧೂತಪಂಕಜಾಯ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೨ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, whose
feet surpass the lotus in beauty, who
grants the wishes of those who
surrender to Him, and who is the
disciple of Sri Paramasivendra

ವಿಜನನದೀಕುಂಜಗೃಹೇ
ಮಂಜುಲಪುಲಿನೈಕಮಂಜುತರತಲ್ಪೇ ।
ಶಯನಂ ಕುರ್ವಾಣಾಯ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೩ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who
reclines on the pleasant bed made of
lovely sands in the bowers of lonely
river-banks.

ಕಾಮಾಹಿದ್ವಿಜಪತಯೇ
ಶಮದಮಮುಖದಿವ್ಯರತ್ನವಾರಿಧಯೇ ।
ಶಮನಾಯ ಮೋಹವಿತತೇಃ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೪ ॥

For the conquest of hosts of delusions,
we salute sri Sadasivendra, who is like
Garuda(the king of birds) to the
Serpent of desires, and who is a
treasure trove of noble qualities like
mind-control and sense-control.

ನಮದಾತ್ಮಬೋಧದಾಯಾ-
ರಮತೇ ಪರಮಾತ್ಮತತ್ತ್ವಸೌಧಾಗ್ರೇ ।
ಸಮಬುದ್ಧಯೇಽಶ್ಮಹೇಮ್ನೋಃ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೫ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who
treats stone and gold alike, and
bestows self-knowledge on those who
take refuge in Him, Himself revelling in
the lofty turrets of the edifice of self-
knowledge.

ಗಿಲಿತಾವಿದ್ಯಾಹಾಲಾ-
ಹಲಹತಪುರ್ಯಷ್ಟಕಾಯ ಬೋಧೇನ ।
ಮೋಹಾಂಧಕಾರರವಯೇ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೬ ॥

Our obeisance to Sri Sadasivendra,
who is like the Sun to the darkness of
delusion, and who, through self-
knowledge, destroys the subtle body
(linga sareera) by swallowing, as it
were, the 'haalahala' venom of neiscience.

ಶಮಮುಖಷಟ್ಕಮುಮುಕ್ಷಾ-
ವಿವೇಕವೈರಾಗ್ಯದಾನನಿರತಾಯ ।
ತರಸಾ ನತಜನತತಯೇ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೭ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who is
intent on quickly granting the four
essential qualifications fo Viveka
(discrimination), Vairaggya
(dispassion), the Hexad of Sama
(mind-control)etc, the Mumukshutva
(desire for liberation) to those who take
refuge in Him.

ಸಿದ್ಧಾಂತ ಕಲ್ಪವಲ್ಲೀ-
ಮುಖಕೃತಿಕರ್ತ್ರೇ ಕಪಾಲಿಭಕ್ತಿಕೃತೇ ।
ಕರತಲಮುಕ್ತಿಫಲಾಯ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೮ ॥

Our obeisance to Sri Sadasivendra,
who is the author of great works like
Siddhanta Kalpavalli, who is a
devotee of Lord Siva, and who holds
the fruit of liberation in His palm.

ತೃಣಪಂಕಲಿಪ್ತವಪುಷೇ
ತೃಣತೋಽಪ್ಯಧರಂ ಜಗದ್ವಿಲೋಕಯತೇ ।
ವನಮಧ್ಯವಿಹರಣಾಯ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಗಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೯ ॥

Our salutations to Sri Sadasivendra,
who wanders inside forests, whose
body is smeared with mud and grass,
and who considers the universe as
even more trivial than grass.

ನಿಗೃಹೀತಹೃದಯಹರಯೇ
ಪ್ರಗೃಹೀತಾತ್ಮಸ್ವರೂಪರತ್ನಾಯ ।
ಪ್ರಣತಾಬ್ಧಿಪೂರ್ಣಶಶಿನೇ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೧೦ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who has
subdued the monkey of His heart, who
has in His hand the precious gem of
self-realisation, and who is like the full
moon to the ocean of devotees.

ಅಜ್ಞಾನತಿಮಿರರವಯೇ
ಪ್ರಜ್ಞಾನಾಮ್ಭೋಧಿಪೂರ್ಣಚನ್ದ್ರಾಯ ।
ಪ್ರಣತಾಘವಿಪಿನಶುಚಯೇ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೧೧ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who is
the sun to the darkness of ignorance,
the full moon to the ocean of self-
knowledge, and fire to the forest of sins
of those who have surrendered to Him.

ಮತಿಮಲಮೋಚನದಕ್ಷ-
ಪ್ರತ್ಯಗ್ಬ್ರಹ್ಮೈಕ್ಯದಾನನಿರತಾಯ ।
ಸ್ಮೃತಿಮಾತ್ರತುಷ್ಟಮನಸೇ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೧೨ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who is
adept in cleansing the sullied intellect
and granting the knowledge of the
unity of the individual soul and
Brahman, and is pleased at even a thought of Him.

ನಿಜಗುರುಪರಮಶಿವೇಂದ್ರ-
ಶ್ಲಾಘಿತವಿಜ್ಞಾನಕಾಷ್ಠಾಯ ।
ನಿಜತತ್ತ್ವನಿಶ್ಚಲಹೃದೇ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೧೩ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, whose
mind is totally tranquil because of self-
realisation, and who got the
appreciation of His Guru, Sri
Paramashivendra for attaining the limit
of knowledge.

ಪ್ರವಿಲಾಪ್ಯ ಜಗದಶೇಷಂ
ಪರಿಶಿಷ್ಟಾಖಣ್ಡವಸ್ತುನಿರತಾಯ ।
ಆಸ್ಯಪ್ರಾಪ್ತಾನ್ನಭುಜೇ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೧೪ ॥

Our salutations to Sri Sadasivendra,
who, having mentally dissolved the
entire universe, is immersed in the
residual 'Atma Tatwa', and who
contentedly part takes of only food that
comes His way.

ಉಪಧಾನೀಕೃತಬಾಹುಃ
ಪರಿರಬ್ಧವಿರಕ್ತಿರಾಮೋ ಯಃ ।
ವಸನೀಕೃತಖಾಯಾಸ್ಮೈ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೧೫ ॥

Our obeisance to Sri Sadasivendra,
who, enjoying the embrace of
dispassion, reposes, having His arm
for a pillow and space for His garment.

ಸಕಲಾಗಮಾಂತಸಾರ-
ಪ್ರಕಟನದಕ್ಷಾಯ ನಮ್ರಪಕ್ಷಾಯ ।
ಸಚ್ಚಿತ್ಸುಖರೂಪಾಯ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೧೬ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who is
adept at revealing the essence of all
the scriptures, is by the side of those
who bow to Him, and is of the nature of
Existence-Consciousness-Bliss.

ದ್ರಾಕ್ಷಾಶಿಕ್ಷಣಚತುರ-
ವ್ಯಾಹಾರಾಯ ಪ್ರಭೂತಕರುಣಾಯ ।
ವೀಕ್ಷಾಪಾವಿತಜಗತೇ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೧೭ ॥

Our salutations to Sri Sadasivendra,
whose utterances can teach
sweetness to sweet grapes, who has
abundant compassion, and whose
glance itself sanctifies the world.

ಯೋಽನುತ್ಪನ್ನವಿಕಾರೋ
ಬಾಹೌ ಮ್ಲೇಚ್ಛೇನ ಚ್ಛಿನ್ನಪತಿತೇಽಪಿ ।
ಅವಿದಿತಮಮತಾಯಾಸ್ಮೈ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ।। ೧೮ ।।

Our obeisance to Sri Sadasivendra,
who had no feeling of 'mine', and who
experienced no mental perturbation
whatsoever even when a wicked man
cut His arm away.

ನ್ಯಪತನ್ಸುಮಾನಿ ಮೂರ್ಧನಿ
ಯೇನೋಚ್ಚರಿತೇಷು ನಾಮಸೂಗ್ರಸ್ಯ ।
ತಸ್ಮೈ ಸಿದ್ಧವರಾಯ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೧೯ ॥

Whenever he uttered the names of
Lord Siva, there would be a shower of
flowers from the heavens. To such a
Siddha par excellence, Sri
Sadasivendra, we submit
our salutations.

ಯಃ ಪಾಪಿನೋಽಪಿ ಲೋಕಾಂ-
ಸ್ತರಸಾ ಪ್ರಕರೋತಿ ಪುಣ್ಯನಿಷ್ಠಾಗ್ರ್ಯಾನ್ ।
ಕರುಣಾಂಬುರಾಶಯೇಽಸ್ಮೈ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ।। ೨೦ ।।

Our obeisance to Sri Sadasivendra,
the veritable ocean of mercy, who
transforms even sinners into the
foremost among virtuous ones.

ಸಿದ್ಧೇಶ್ವರಾಯ ಬುದ್ಧೇಃ
ಶುದ್ಧಿಪ್ರದಪಾದಪದ್ಮನಮನಾಯ ।
ಬದ್ಧೌಘಮೋಚಕಾಯ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೨೧ ॥

Salutations to Sri Sadasivendra,
the king of Siddhas, who grants purity of
mind to those who bow at His lotus
feet, and liberates hosts of men in
bondage.

ಹೃದ್ಯಾಯ ಲೋಕವಿತತೇಃ
ಪದ್ಯಾವಲಿದಾಯ ಜನ್ಮಮೂಕೇಭ್ಯಃ ।
ಪ್ರಣತೇಭ್ಯಃ ಪದಯುಗಲೇ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೨೨ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who is
dear to the heart of multitudes, and
who grants poetic ability to even those
born dumb, who fall at His pair of feet.

ಜಿಹ್ವೋಪಸ್ಥರತಾನ-
ಪ್ಯಾಹೋಚ್ಚಾರೇಣ ಜಾತು ನೈಜಸ್ಯ ।
ಕುರ್ವಾಣಾಯ ವಿರಕ್ತಾ-
ನ್ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೨೩ ॥

The mere utterance of His name
converts those revelling in the senses
into ones established in dispassion.
We salute Him, Sri Sadasivendra.

ಕಮನೀಯಕವನಕರ್ತ್ರೇ
ಶಮನೀಯಭಯಾಪಹಾರಚತುರಾಯ ।
ತಪನೀಯಸದೃಶವಪುಷೇ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೨೪ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, whose
body is as resplendent as molten gold,
who is an expert allayer of the fear of
death, and who is the creator of
enchanting poetry.

ತಾರಕವಿದ್ಯಾದಾತ್ರೇ
ತಾರಾಪತಿಗರ್ವವಾರಕಾಸ್ಯಾಯ ।
ತಾರಜಪಪ್ರವಣಾಯ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೨೫ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who
gives knowledge that enables one to
cross the ocean of transmigratory
existence, whose face surpasses the
moon in beauty, and who is adept at
chanting the Omkara.

ಮೂಕೋಽಪಿ ಯತ್ಕೃಪಾ ಚೇ-
ಲ್ಲೋಕೋತ್ತರಕೀರ್ತಿರಾಶು ಜಾಯೇತ ।
ಅದ್ಭುತಚರಿತಾಯಾಸ್ಮೈ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ।। ೨೬ ।।

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, whose
life story is a source of wonderment,
and whose grace can catapult even a
mute person into world renown.

ದುರ್ಜನದೂರಾಯತರಾಂ
ಸಜ್ಜನಸುಲಭಾಯ ಪಾತ್ರಹಸ್ತಾಯ ।
ತರುತಲನಿಕೇತನಾಯ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೨೭ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who is
the farthest removed from the wicked,
is easily accessible to the good, whose
cupped hands serve as the only vessel
He possesses, and whose abode is
the foot of a tree.

ಭವಸಿಂಧುತಾರಯಿತ್ರೇ
ಭವಭಕ್ತಾಯ ಪ್ರಣಮ್ರವಶ್ಯಾಯ ।
ಭವಬಂಧವಿರಹಿತಾಯ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೨೮ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who had
no bondage of Samsara, and makes
others cross the ocean of Samsara,
who is an ardent devotee of Lord
Bhava (Siva), and who is at the
ommand of His true devotees.

ತ್ರಿವಿಧಸ್ಯಾಪಿ ತ್ಯಾಗಂ
ವಪುಷಃ ಕರ್ತುಂ ಸ್ಥಲತ್ರಯೇ ಯ ಇವ ।
ಅಕರೋತ್ಸಮಾಧಿಮಸ್ಮೈ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ।। ೨೯ ।।

We submit our obeisance to Sri
Sadasivendra, who sat in
Samadhi in three places simultaneously (Nerur,
Manamadurai and Kasi) for
renouncing the three bodies (the
gross, the subtle and the causal).

ಕಾಮಿನಮಪಿ ಜಿತಹೃದಯಂ
ಕ್ರೂರಂ ಶಾಂತಂ ಜಡಂ ಸುಧಿಯಮ್ ।
ಕುರುತೇ ಯತ್ಕರುಣಾಸ್ಮೈ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೩೦ ॥

Our salutations to Sri Sadasivendra,
whose compassion makes a sensuous
person self-controlled, a cruel person
serene and a dullard highly intelligent.

ವೇದಸ್ಮೃತಿಸ್ಥವಿದ್ವ-
ಲಕ್ಷಣಲಕ್ಷ್ಯೇಷು ಸಂದಿಹಾನಾನಾಮ್ ।
ನಿಶ್ಚಯಕೃತೇ ವಿಹರ್ತ್ರೇ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೩೧ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who
roams about clearing the doubts of
people regarding scriptural
descriptions of the characteristics and
goals of realised ones.

ಬಾಲಾರುಣನಿಭವಪುಷೇ
ಲೀಲಾನಿರ್ಧೂತಕಾಮಗರ್ವಾಯ ।
ಲೋಲಾಯ ಚಿತಿ ಪರಸ್ಯಾಂ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೩೨ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, whose
body is resplendent as the rising sun,
who destroys Cupid's pride as if in play.
and who is established in the Supreme
Consciousness.

ಶರಣೀಕೃತಾಯ ಸುಗುಣೈ-
ಶ್ಚರಣೀಕೃತರಕ್ತಪಂಕಜಾತಾಯ ।
ಧರಣೀಸಹಕ್ಕ್ಷಮಾಯ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೩೩ ॥

All noble qualities have taken refuge in
Him, as it were, and it is as though He
has adopted red lotuses as His feet.
To such a one, Sri Sadasivendra, who
equals the Earth in forbearance, we
offer our salutations.

ಪ್ರಣತಾಯ ಯತಿವರೇಣ್ಯೈ-
ರ್ಗಣನಾಥೇನಾಪ್ಯಹಾರ್ಯವಿಘ್ನಹೃತೇ ।
ಗುಣದಾಸೀಕೃತಜಗತೇ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೩೪ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who is
saluted by the best of ascetics, who
removes obstacles that cannot be
removed even by Lord Vighneshwara,
and whose noble qualities have made
the entire world subservient to Him.

ಸಹಮಾನಾಯ ಸಹಸ್ರಾ-
ಣ್ಯಪ್ಯಪರಾಧಾನ್ ಪ್ರಣಮ್ರಜನರಚಿತಾನ್ ।
ಸಹಸೈವ ಮೋಕ್ಷದಾತ್ರೇ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೩೫ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who
forgives even thousands of offences
committed by those who bow to Him,
and who gives liberation instantly.

ಧೃತದೇಹಾಯ ನತಾವಲಿ-
ತೂರ್ಣಪ್ರಜ್ಞಾಪ್ರದಾನವಾಂಛಾತಃ ।
ಶ್ರೀದಕ್ಷಿಣವಕ್ತ್ರಾಯ
ಪ್ರಣತಿಂ ಕುರ್ಮಃ ಸದಾಶಿವೇಂದ್ರಾಯ ॥ ೩೬ ॥

We salute Sri Sadasivendra, who is
verily Lord Dakshinamurthi, who has
taken a human form owing to His
desire to grant self-knowledge quickly
to those who surrender to Him.

ತಾಪತ್ರಯಾರ್ತಹೃದಯ-
ಸ್ತಾಪತ್ರಯಹಾರದಕ್ಷನಮನಮಹಮ್ ।
ಗುರುವರಬೋಧಿತಮಹಿಮಾ
ಶರಣಂ ಯಾಸ್ಯೇ ತವಾಂಘ್ರಿಕಮಲಯುಗಮ್ ॥ ೩೭ ॥

Having a heart tormented by the three
kinds of miseries (viz. adhyaatma,
aadhibhowtika and aadhidaivika), and
having come to know of Your
greatness through the pre-eminent
Guru, I surrender at Your pair of lotus
feet, that destroy the miseries of those
who bow to You.

ಸದಾತ್ಮನಿ ವಿಲೀನಹೃತ್ಸಕಲವೇದಶಾಸ್ತ್ರಾರ್ಥವಿತ್
ಸರಿತ್ತಟವಿಹಾರಕೃತ್ಸಕಲಲೋಕಹೃತ್ತಾಪಹೃತ್ ।
ಸದಾಶಿವಪದಾಂಬುಜಪ್ರಣತಲೋಕಲಭ್ಯ ಪ್ರಭೋ
ಸದಾಶಿವಯತೀಟ್ ಸದಾ ಮಯಿ ಕೃಪಾಮಪಾರಾಂ ಕುರು ॥ ೩೮ ॥

O Lord! Your heart is always immersed
in the Atman. You are well-versed in
the scriptures and their meanings. You
destroy the afflictions in the hearts of
all. You roam the banks of rivers, and
are accessible to those who bow to the
lotus feet of Lord Siva. O Sadasiva, the
Lord of ascetics! Shower Your limitless
compassion on Me.

ಪುರಾ ಯವನಕರ್ತನಸ್ರವದಮಂದರಕ್ತೋಽಪಿ ಯಃ
ಪುನಃ ಪದಸರೋರುಹಪ್ರಣತಮೇನಮೇನೋನಿಧಿಮ್ ।
ಕೃಪಾಪರವಶಃ ಪದಂ ಪತನವರ್ಜಿತಂ ಪ್ರಾಪಯ-
ತ್ಸದಾಶಿವಯತೀಟ್ ಸ ಮಯ್ಯನವಧಿಂ ಕೃಪಾಂ ಸಿಞ್ಚತು ॥ ೩೯ ॥

Out of His extreme compassion, Sri
Sadasiva granted liberation to the
wicked man, a veritable repository of
all sins when he remorsefully fell at the
Sage's lotus feet after causing profuse
bleeding by cutting off His arm. May
that Lord of ascetics shower His
limitless compassion on Me.

ಹೃಷೀಕಹತಚೇತಸಿ ಪ್ರಹೃತದೇಹಕೇ ರೋಗಕೈ-
ರನೇಕವೃಜಿನಾಲಯೇ ಶಮದಮಾದಿಗನ್ಧೋಜ್ಝಿತೇ ।
ತವಾಂಘ್ರಿಪತಿತೇ ಯತೌ ಯತಿಪತೇ ಮಹಾಯಾಂಗಿರಾಟ್
ಸದಾಶಿವ ಕೃಪಾಂ ಮಯಿ ಪ್ರಕುರು ಹೇತುಶೂನ್ಯಾಂ ದ್ರುತಮ್ ॥ ೪೦ ॥

O Lord of great yogin-s! I, who am the
abode of numerous sins, an ascetic
with a heart tormented by the senses,
with a body afflicted by disease, and
without even a trace of noble qualities
like mind-control and sense-control,
have fallen at Your feet. O Protector of
ascetics! Shower Your unconditional
compassion on Me.

ನ ಚಾಹಮತಿಚಾತುರೀರಚಿತಶಬ್ದಸಂಘೈಃ ಸ್ತುತಿಂ
ವಿಧಾತುಮಪಿ ಚ ಕ್ಷಮೋ ನ ಚ ಜಪಾದಿಕೇಽಪ್ಯಸ್ತಿ ಮೇಂ |
ಬಲಂ ಬಲವತಾಂ ವರ ಪ್ರಕುರು ಹೇತುಶೂನ್ಯಾಂ ವಿಭೋ
ಸದಾಶಿವ ಕೃಪಾಂ ಮಯಿ ಪ್ರವರ ಯೋಗಿನಾಂ ಸತ್ವರಮ್ ॥ ೪೧ ॥

O Sadasiva, the greatest of yogis! O
strongest of the strong! O omnipresent
one! Quickly bestow unconditional
compassion on Me, who has no ability
to sing Your praises with brilliant
words, or the strength for japa, etc.

ಶಬ್ದಾರ್ಥವಿಜ್ಞಾನಯುತಾ ಹಿ ಲೋಕೇ
ವಸಂತಿ ಲೋಕಾ ಬಹವಃ ಪ್ರಕಾಮಮ್ ।
ನಿಷ್ಟಾಯುತಾ ನ ಶ್ರುತದೃಷ್ಟಪೂರ್ವಾ
ವಿನಾ ಭವಂತಂ ಯತಿರಾಜ ನೂನಮ್ ॥ ೪೨ ॥

In this world there are any number of
people who have the knowledge of the
scriptural utterances. But, O king
among ascetics! Other than You, there
is certainly none seen or heard of, who
is established in the import of the
scriptures.

ಸ್ತೋಕಾರ್ಚನಪ್ರೀತಹೃದಂಬುಜಾಯ
ಪಾಕಾಬ್ಜಚೂಡಾಪರರೂಪಧರ್ತ್ರೇ ।
ಶೋಕಾಪಹರ್ತ್ರೇ ತರಸಾ ನತಾನಾಂ
ಪಾಕಾಯ ಪುಣ್ಯಸ್ಯ ನಮೋ ಯತೀಶೇ ॥ ೪೩ ॥

Salutations to the king of ascetics,
whose heart is well-pleased even with
a little worship, who quickly removes
the miseries of those who bow to Him,
who is the fruit of all merits, and who is
but another form of the Lord, who
wears the crescent moon (Lord Siva).

ನಾಹಂ ಹೃಷೀಕಾಣಿ ವಿಜೇತುಮೀಶೋ
ನಾಹಂ ಸಪರ್ಯಾಭಜನಾದಿ ಕರ್ತುಮ್ ।
ನಿಸರ್ಗಯಾ ತ್ವಂ ದಯಯೈವ ಪಾಹಿ
ಸದಾಶಿವೇಮಂ ಕರುಣಾಪಯೋಧೇ ॥ ೪೪ ॥

O Sadasiva! o ocean of mercy! With
compassion so natural to You, save
Me, who am incapable of controlling
My senses, and of performing any
worship.

ಕೃತಯಾನಯಾ ನತಾವಲಿ-
ಕೋಟಿಗತೇನಾತಿಮಂದಬೋಧೇನ ।
ಮುದಮೇಹಿ ನಿತ್ಯತೃಪ್ತ-
ಪ್ರವರ ಸ್ತುತ್ಯಾ ಸದಾಶಿವಾಶ್ವಾಶು ॥ ೪೫ ॥

O Sadasiva, the foremost among the
ever-contented! Be pleased quickly
with this hymn composed by this
extremely dull witted one, who is the
most insignificant of all Your devotees.
