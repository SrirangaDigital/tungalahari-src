॥ śrīśambhustavaḥ ॥

kailāsaśailanilayātkalikalmaṣaghnā-
ccandrārdhabhūṣitajaṭādvaṭamūlavāsāt ।
namrottamāṅgaviniveśitahastapadmā-
cchambhoḥ paraṁ kimapi daivamahaṁ na jāne ॥ 1 ॥

nākādhināthakarapallavasevitāṅghre-
rnāgāsyaṣaṇmukhavibhāsitapārśvabhāgāt ।
nirvyājapūrṇakaruṇānnikhilāmareḍyā-
cchambhoḥ paraṁ kimapi daivamahaṁ na jāne ॥ 2 ॥

maunīndrarakṣaṇakṛte jitakālagarvā-
tpāpābdhiśoṣaṇavidhau jitavāḍavāgneḥ ।
mārāṅgabhasmaparilepanaśuklagātrā-
cchambhoḥ paraṁ kimapi daivamahaṁ na jāne ॥ 3 ॥

vijñānamudritakarāccharadinduśubhrā-
dvijñānadānaniratājjaḍapaṅktaye'pi ।
vedāntageyacaraṇādvidhiviṣṇusevyā-
cchambhoḥ paraṁ kimapi daivamahaṁ na jāne ॥ 4 ॥
