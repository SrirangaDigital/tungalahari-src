॥ śrīśivastutikadambam ॥

āhlādajanakasyādya sānnidhyāttava śaṅkara
candraścandratvamāpede jāne candralasajjaṭa ॥ 1 ॥

kālakūṭaṁ nigṛhyādāvarakṣaḥ sakalaṁ jagat ।
ko vātra vismayaḥ śambho kālasyaikasya nigrahe ॥ 2 ॥

abhavastvaṁ sūcayituṁ lokānāmardhanārīśaḥ ।
ardho vetyāmnāyaḥ svārthaparo nārthavāda iti ॥ 3 ॥

jaḍatāvidalanadīkṣita jaḍatāpahṛtiṁ karoṣi no cenme ।
dīkṣābhaṅgo na bhaveddākṣāyaṇyāśritāṅga kimu tena ॥ 4 ॥

paśupatimava māṁ śambho paśupatirasi giriśa yasmāttvam ।
śrutirapyevaṁ brūte kartavyā hyātmarakṣeti ॥ 5 ॥

śīrṣopari candraste loke śāstre ca vikhyātaḥ ।
kaṇṭhoparyakalaṅkaḥ pūrṇaḥ ko'yaṁ niśākaro brūhi ॥ 6 ॥

kavitvavārāśiśaranniśeśaṁ
jaḍatvanāgrendavibhedasiṁham ।
mṛgatvagābaddhakaṭipradeśaṁ
mahatvadaṁ naumi natāya śambhum ॥ 7 ॥

yadaṅghripāthoruhasevanena
prayāti sarvottamatāṁ jaḍo'pi ।
tamambikāmānasapadmahaṁsa-
mupāśraye satvaracittaśuddhyai ॥ 8 ॥

bahūnāṁ janānāṁ mano'bhīṣṭajātaṁ
susūkṣmaṁ vitīryāśu garvāyase tvam ।
maheśāna yadyasti śaktistavāho
mahanmanmano'bhīṣṭamāśu prayaccha ॥ 9 ॥

apāṁ puṣpārdhasya pratidinamaho dhāraṇavaśāt
prabho kiṁ nirvedāddharaṇigatapuṣpālimadhunā ।
rasāddhatse śīrṣe śaśadharakirīṭāgatanayā-
sahāya prabrūhi praṇatajanakāruṇyabharita ॥ 10 ॥

bahoḥ kālātkiṁ vā śirasi kṛtavāsaṁ tava vidhuṁ
viyogaṁ kiṁ patyurbhṛśamasahamānāḥ svayamaho ।
samāliṅgantyetāḥ patimatirasātpuṣpamiṣataḥ
prabho tārāstasmādasi sumakirīṭastvamadhunā ॥ 11 ॥

bhāktānāṁ hṛdrathānāṁ nijanijapadavīprāptaye pārvatīśaḥ
kāruṇyāpāravārānnidhiragapatijāsaṁyutaḥ sambhrameṇa ।
āruhyaikaṁ hi bāhyaṁ rathamiha nikhilāṁścālayankiṁ puroktān
garvaṁ pakṣīśavāyvorharati karuṇayā śīghranamreṣṭadāyī ॥ 12 ॥

matpāpānāṁ bahūnāṁ parimitiradhunādhīśa nāstyeva nūnaṁ
tvadvatpāpopaśāntipradamiha bhuvane nāsti daivaṁ ca sadyaḥ ।
tasmānmatpāparāśiṁ daha daha tarasā dehi śuddhāṁ ca buddhiṁ
srotaḥ śreṣṭhāvataṁsa praṇatabhayahara prāṇanāthāgajāyāḥ ॥ 13 ॥

kāmaṁ santu surāḥ svapādanamanastotrārcanābhiściraṁ
dehaṁ karśayate janāya phaladāstānnāśraye jātvapi ।
yo jātvapyavaśātsvanāma vadate lokāya śīghreṣṭadaḥ
so'vyāddhetuvihīnapūrṇakaruṇaḥ kāntāyitārdhaḥ śivaḥ ॥ 14 ॥

nityānityavivekabhogaviratī śāntyādiṣaṭkaṁ tathā
mokṣecchāmanapāyinīṁ vitara bho śambho kṛpāvāridhe ।
vedāntaśravaṇaṁ tadarthamananaṁ dhyānaṁ ciraṁ brahmaṇaḥ
saccidrūpatanorakhaṇḍaparamānandātmanaḥ śaṅkara ॥ 15 ॥

mannīkāśatanuṁ pragṛhya karuṇāvārāṁnidhe satvaraṁ
śṛṅgādrau vasa modataḥ karuṇayā vyākhyānasiṁhāsane ।
kurvaṁllokatatiṁ svadharmaniratāṁ saukhyairaśeṣairvṛtā-
madvaitātmavibodhapūrṇahṛdayāṁ cātanvaparṇāpate ॥ 16 ॥

yatpādāmbujasamarcanasaktāḥ
saktimāśu viṣayeṣu vihāya ।
saccidātmani vilīnamanaskāḥ
sambhavanti tamahaṁ śivamīḍe ॥ 17 ॥

rajanīvallabhacūḍo
rajanīcarasevyapadapadmaḥ ।
rākāśaśāṅkadhavalo
rājati ramaṇīgṛhītavāmāṅgaḥ ॥ 18 ॥

karavāṇītanubhiste
karavāṇīśāṅghrisannatiṁ modāt ।
karavāṇītanuśuddhyai
karavāṇīśrībahutvāya ॥ 19 ॥
