॥ śrīkanyākumārīpañcaratnastutiḥ ॥

kāntidhūtakanakākhilagarvāṁ
kāñcanāśmasamabuddhividhātrīm ।
kāṅkṣitārthalaghudānasamarthāṁ
kāñcanātra kalaye hṛdi kanyām ॥ 1 ॥

mānyamaṅgajariporapi yatta-
cchūnyadehamatimukhyavikalpaiḥ ।
manyate jaḍajano hṛdi kurve
kanyakāmahamaghaughanivṛttyai ॥ 2 ॥

vanyamūlaphalarakṣitadehai-
rjanyasaukhyavimukhairupaśāntaiḥ ।
kanyakāṁ dharapaterupasevyā-
manyaśūnyahṛdayaḥ praṇato'smi ॥ 3 ॥

kumārīṁ ramāmukhyadevāṅganārcyāṁ
samādhipravīṇaiḥ samārādhyapādām ।
kṣamāgarvamātma kṣamāto'pidūraṁ
nayantīṁ śamādīnabhīpsannamasye ॥ 4 ॥

kṣapānāthacūḍāṁ kṛpāvārirāśiṁ
vipāpeḍyapādāmapāraṁ bhavābdhim ।
svapādābjanamrāndrutaṁ tārayantīṁ
kumārīṁ bhaje kanyapūrvāmajasram ॥ 5 ॥
