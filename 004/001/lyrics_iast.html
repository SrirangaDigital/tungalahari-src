॥ śrī gurudhyānam ॥

ajñānadhvāntavidhvaṁsisahasrakaratejasam ।
saccitsukhātmakabrahmaniviṣṭahṛdayāmbujam ॥ 1 ॥

padmāsanopaviṣṭañca dhyānastimitalocanam ।
samaṁ kāyaśirogrīvaṁ dhārayantaṁ mahāprabham ॥ 2 ॥

sahasrakiraṇaspardhidehakāntisamujjvalam ।
nirvikalpasamādhisthaṁ niścalāvayavaṁ śivam ॥ 3 ॥

dehādibrahmaparyante bhogye tucchatvadhīyutam ।
ārtānāmārtihantāraṁ jijñāsuviśayacchidam ॥ 4 ॥

svāvalokanamātreṇa punānaṁ nikhilaṁ jagat ।
madīyahṛtsarojātaniveśitapadadvayam ।
vidyātīrthagurūttaṁsaṁ dhyāyāmi bhavamuktaye ॥ 5 ॥
