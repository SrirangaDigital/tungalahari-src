॥ śrīkaṇṭhāṣṭakam ॥

yaḥ pādapapihitatanuḥ
prakāśatāṁ paraśurāmeṇa ।
nītaḥ so'vyātsatataṁ
śrīkaṇṭhaḥ pādanamrakalpataruḥ ॥ 1 ॥

yaḥ kālaṁ jitagarvaṁ
kṛtvā kṣaṇato mṛkaṇḍumunisūnum ।
nirbhayamakarotso'vyāt
śrīkaṇṭhaḥ pādanamrakalpataruḥ ॥ 2 ॥

kuṣṭhāpasmāramukhā
rogā yatpādasevanātsahasā ।
praśamaṁ prayānti so'vyāt
śrīkaṇṭhaḥ pādanamrakalpataruḥ ॥ 3 ॥

yadavidyayaiva jagadida-
makhilaṁ pratibhāti satyavatpūrvam ।
jñānātso'vyātsatataṁ
śrīkaṇṭhaḥ pādanamrakalpataruḥ ॥ 4 ॥

yamibṛndavandyacaraṇaḥ
kamitā dharaṇīdharendratanayāyāḥ ।
śrīśādivandito'vyāt
śrīkaṇṭhaḥ pādanamrakalpataruḥ ॥ 5 ॥

yo dakṣiṇāsyarūpaṁ
dhṛtvā vijñānadānakṛtadīkṣaḥ ।
magdhebhyo'pi sa pāyāt
śrīkaṇṭhaḥ pādanamrakalpataruḥ ॥ 6 ॥

andho'pi yatkaruṇayā
cakṣuṣmānbhavati satvaraṁ loke ।
karuṇānidhiḥ sa pāyāt
śrīkaṇṭhaḥ pādanamrakalpataruḥ ॥ 7 ॥

kapilātaṭādṛtagatiḥ
kapilādimunīndravandyapadapadmaḥ ।
śrīdaḥ pāyātsatataṁ
śrīkaṇṭhaḥ pādanamrakalpataruḥ ॥ 8 ॥

śrīkaṇṭhāṣṭakameta-
tpaṭhati jano yaḥ kṛtādaraḥ satatam ।
śrīvidyāsadanaṁ sa
prabhavennaivātra sandehaḥ ॥ 9 ॥
