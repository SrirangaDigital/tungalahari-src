॥ śrīmadṛṣyaśṛṅgeśvarastutiḥ ॥

kaṣṭārivargadalanaṁ
śiṣṭālisamarcitāṅghripāthojam ।
naṣṭāvidyairgamyaṁ
puṣṭātmārādhakālimākalaye ॥ 1 ॥

prāṇāyāmaidhryānai-
rnaṣṭaṁ cittaṁ vidhāya munivaryāḥ ।
yatpaśyanti hṛdabje
śāntābhāgyaṁ namāmi tatkiñcit ॥ 2 ॥

vedottamāṅgageyaṁ
nādopāstyādisādhanātmākhyam ।
khedonmūlanadakṣaṁ
bhedopādhyādivarjitaṁ naumi ॥ 3 ॥

śāntāmānasahaṁsaṁ
kāntārāsaktamunivaraiḥsevyam ।
śāntāhaṅkṛtivedyaṁ
kāntārdhaṁ naumi śṛṅgaśivam ॥ 4 ॥
