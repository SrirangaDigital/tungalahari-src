॥ śrīdevīdhāmāṣṭakam ॥

kasmaicidaṅghripraṇatākhileṣṭa-
viśrāṇanavrīḍitakaustubhāya ।
kāmārivāmāṅkajuṣe kirīṭa-
kanacchaśāṅkāya namo'stu dhāmne ॥ 1 ॥

kasmaicidudyadravikoṭibhāse
kalpadrumāṇāmapi garvahartre ।
puṇḍrekṣupāśāṅkuśapuṣpabāṇa-
hastāya śastāya namo'stu dhāmne ॥ 2 ॥

kasmaicidādyāya namo'stu dhāmne
bandhūkapuṣpābhakalebarāya ।
kulādrivaṁśāmbudhikaustubhāya
mattebhakumbhastanabandhurāya ॥ 3 ॥

kasmaicidādyāya namo'stu dhāmne
bhaṇḍāsurāmbhonidhibāḍabāya ।
bhaktaughasaṁrakṣaṇadakṣiṇāya
bhādhīśanīkāśamukhāmbujāya ॥ 4 ॥

kasmaicidastu praṇatiḥ karāmbu-
jātamradimnā hasate pravālam ।
kāruṇyajanmāvanaye kapardi-
modābdhirākārajanīkarāya ॥ 5 ॥

kalyāṇaśailādhipamadhyaśṛṅga-
niketanāya praṇatārtihantre ।
kravyādavairipramukheḍitāya
kurmaḥ praṇāmaṁ kutukāya śambhau ॥ 6 ॥

kacaprabhānirjitanīradāya
kastūrikākuṅkumalepanāya ।
bimbādharāya śrutibodhitāya
bandhāpanodāya namo'stu dhāmne ॥ 7 ॥

kaṭākṣakāṅkṣipravarāmarāya
kālāricittāmbujabhāskarāya ।
paṭīyase pāpasamūhabhede
namo'stu kasmaicidamoghadhāmne ॥ 8 ॥
