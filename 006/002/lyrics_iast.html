॥ śrīgaṇādhipastutiḥ ॥

kukṣisphurannāgabhītyā mūṣikaḥ prapalāyate ।
iti matvā mayūraṁ kimārūḍho'si gaṇādhipa ॥ 1 ॥

matkukṣisthamakhilaṁ jagaditi sarvān prabodhayitum ।
bṛhadudaratāṁ kṛpābdhe dhatse kiṁ karivarāsya tvam ॥ 2 ॥

tvaddarśanakṛtahāsaṁ candramasaṁ hantumudyuktam ।
tvāmanunetuṁ tārāḥ kusumavyājena sevante ॥ 3 ॥

yaccāturyeṇaiva hi lokānuddhartumambikānāthaḥ ।
gokarṇe sthitimakarollokahitaṁ taṁ praṇaumi vighneśam ॥ 4 ॥
