॥ navaratnamālā ॥

lokaṁ samastamapi mohamahāndhakāra-
naśyaddṛśaṁ bhavamahājaladhau patantam ।
soktyañjanena samapālayadāśu yaḥ saḥ
dṛggocaro bhavatu deśikarāṇmamāśu ॥ 1 ॥

pāṣaṇḍamukhyamatakumbhiṣu kesarīndraḥ
pāpāṭavīdavavibhāvasurānatānām ।
advaitavārinidhipūrṇaśaśī ca yaḥ saḥ
dṛggocaro bhavatu deśikarāṇmamāśu ॥ 2 ॥

śabdādimānhi viṣayānviṣatulyakakṣyā-
njñātvāpi tatra bahurāgavato mamāśu ।
pādānatākhilajanasya viraktidātā
dṛggocaro bhavatu deśikasārvabhaumaḥ ॥ 3 ॥

śrīmannṛsiṁhagurupādayugābjabhṛṅgaḥ
sīmāvilaṅghidhiṣaṇo'khilaśāstravettā ।
namrāntarāndhyavinivāraṇacaṇḍaraśmiḥ
dṛggocaro bhavatu deśikarāṇmamāśu ॥ 4 ॥

kāmādiśrṛṅgiparibhedanavajrapāṇiḥ
nāmāvadhūtanikhilāghacayo mahasvī ।
sāmādiśīrṣaparigītanijasvarūpaḥ
dṛggocaro bhavatu deśikarāṇmamāśu ॥ 5 ॥

āśrāmavāsivaṭumekamabhāṣamāṇaṁ
saṁbhāṣamāṇamakarottvaritaṁ kṛpātaḥ ।
śrīśaṁkarārya iva yaḥ sa hi hastadhātrīṁ
dṛggocaro bhavatu deśikarāṇmamāśu ॥ 6 ॥

vipraṁ hyapasmṛtiyutaṁ prasamīkṣya tasya
mūrdhnyaṅghrimādadhadapārakṛpāmburāśiḥ ।
yo dakṣiṇāsya iva rogamapānudattaṁ
dṛggocaro bhavatu deśikarāṇmamāśu ॥ 7 ॥

ūrdhvasthalānnipatitāṁ gṛhagodhikāṁ yaḥ
tyaktāsumāśu karakāmbukaṇena siktvā ।
so'jīvayajjanasamakṣamameyaśaktiḥ
dṛggocaro bhavatu deśikarāṇmamāśu ॥ 8 ॥

martyācikitsyabahukuṣṭhanipīḍitaṁ yaḥ
vipraṁ hi kaṁcana tadāmayahīnamāśu ।
cakre sa datta iva kevalabhasmadānāt
dṛggocaro bhavatu deśikarāṇmamāśu ॥ 9 ॥
