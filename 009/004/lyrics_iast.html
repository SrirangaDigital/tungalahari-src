॥ śrīmadācāryendrastavam ॥

dṛṣṭvā vāci sarasvatīṁ nayanayorante ca lakṣmīṁ tathā
citte vīkṣya samastabhūtakaruṇāṁ kāye ca hemaprabhām ।
no yuktaṁ bahubhāminīsamudaye vāsāya mattveti te
śrīmaddeśikarāja kīrtivanitā nūnaṁ digantānagāt ॥ 1 ॥

purā śaṁbhuḥ śubhre vasatimacale kartumaniśaṁ
samicchanrūpyādrāvubhayaguṇayukte'kṛta vasim ।
tatastaṁ durvarṇaṁ daśavadanahastapracalitaṁ
vicintyāsminnityāṁ vasatimakaroddeśikahṛdi ॥ 2 ॥

amṛtaśilāparinirmitamūrtiṁ śrīdeśikasya manye'ham ।
śaṁkaragurukṛtaśrṛṅgācalasthapīṭhīyakīrtisāra iti ॥ 3 ॥

ekādaśarudrāṇāmekā gaurī bhavennālam ।
iti gaurīkṛtavāṁstvaṁ deśikavara daśa diśo'pi nijakīrtyā ॥ 4 ॥

prahlādapoṣakatvaṁ tava siddhamatho hiraṇyavairitvam ।
tasmānnṛsiṁhadeśika hṛtstambhaṁ me vibhidya pura ehi ॥ 5 ॥

sarvajñatāsakalaśaktiyutatvahetoḥ
śrīśaṁbhusāmyamakhilā bhavato vadanti ।
ādhikyameva tava deśika saṁgire'haṁ
rājāṅghrirasti hi bhavān sa tu rājamauliḥ ॥ 6 ॥
