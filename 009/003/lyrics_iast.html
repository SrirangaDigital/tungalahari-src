॥ guruprārthanāstotram ॥

pṛthvīmaṇḍalarājamānanṛpamaulyābhātkirīṭojjvala-
dratnābhāñcitapādapadmayugalaśrīmadguro satvaram ।
tvatpādaikaśaraṇyamandamataye'muṣmai janāya prabho
hastālambanamādarādvitara māṁ duḥsaṁsṛtestāraya ॥ 1 ॥

śrīmacchaṁkaradeśikendraracite pīṭhe jagaddeśika
tvādṛkṣāḥ śivarūpiṇo'khilavido brahmasthitā vartitum ।
yuktaṁ ko'hamatīva mandadhiṣaṇo bāhyeṣu sakto'dhunā
sthātuṁ tadbhagavānbhavānkaruṇayā māmuddharatvañjasā ॥ 2 ॥

nityānityavivekaviraktiśamādimamumukṣutāhīnam ।
dṛṣṭvā kṛpārdradṛṣṭyā muktyadhvani pathikamātanuṣva guro ॥ 3 ॥

jñānetareṣṭadāne caturāḥ kalpadrumaṇimukhāḥ santuḥ ।
lokatraye'pi satyaṁ jñānadavastu tvayā vinā nāsti ॥ 4 ॥

pāpīyāniti matvā māṁ yadi lokaikadeśika tyajasi ।
sarvajanatyaktamimaṁ ko vā paripālayetkṛpājaladhe ॥ 5 ॥

tvatpadāmbujayugalaṁ hitvā nānyā gatirhi me loke ।
iti matvā tava caraṇau śaraṇaṁ prāpto'smi karuṇayā rakṣa ॥ 6 ॥

nāstikyatamasi loke sarvajanānāṁ hṛdambujātāni ।
malinīkurvati dharme prāyo naṣṭe sadāśivo bhagavān ॥ 7 ॥

gururūpeṇa dharāyāmavatīryākhilajanānavanvipadaḥ ।
dhikkṛtasūryamahobhirnāstikyatamo vināśya durvāram ॥ 8 ॥

kṛtvā'nekāmānuṣakāryāṇi janaiḥ samarcito nikhilaiḥ ।
tadviditaśaṁkarāṁśo lebhe svaṁ dhāma khalu visṛṣṭāṅgaḥ ॥ 9 ॥

tattādṛṅbhāhimānaṁ lokānugrahanibaddhadīkṣamaham ।
vairāgyadānanirataṁ śaraṇaṁ karavāṇi deśikaṁ bhaktyā ॥ 10 ॥

yannaisargikakaruṇāleśamanuprāpya mānavā jagati ।
svīyācāraratāḥ syustaṁ deśikavaryamādarānnaumi ॥ 11 ॥

śrīmaddeśikavaryānvande'nudinaṁ śivābhinavapūrvān ।
saccitsukharūpānsadvidyāṁ dadataḥ svapādanamrebhyaḥ ॥ 12 ॥
