॥ pādāvalambanastutiḥ ॥

sthite'nukampāmakarandapūrṇe
jagadguroḥ pādasahasrapatre ।
kimanyato dhāvasi cittabhṛṅga
prayāhi tatraiva ramasva nityam ॥

yogivaryayamirājanatāṅghre
bhogirājanavanīyacaritra ।
rāgibhinnamanujāpyapadābja
deśikendra mama dehyavalambam ॥ 2 ॥

yadvaco madhurimāpajitemaṁ
lokameva visasarja sudhā'pi ।
pādapadmanatamuktida sa tvaṁ
dehi deśika mamāṅ‍ghryavalambam ॥ 3 ॥

dātṛbhāvamavagamya mahāntaṁ
karṇa āpa janakarṇapathaṁ te ।
dāritākhiladaridrajanārte
dehi deśika mamāṅ‍ghryavalambam ॥ 4 ॥

śāntidāntiviratīrapavarga-
prāpikāḥ sudṛḍhamāśu vitīrya ।
janmamṛtyubhayarogayutasya
dehi deśika mamāṅ‍ghryavalambam ॥ 5 ॥

darśanātkila duradhvagamartyāḥ
yasya jātu bahavaḥ kṛtakṛtyāḥ ।
sūktitastu kimu vācyamayaṁ tvaṁ
dehi deśika mamāṅ‍ghryavalambam ॥ 6 ॥

bhasmadānaparipūritanamrā-
kaṅkṣitārtha paramātmavidagrya ।
vismayāvahaturīyakayātra
dehi deśika mamāṅ‍ghryavalambam ॥ 7 ॥

īhitaṁ vitara saccitisaṁjña
mohitasya jagadīśvaraśaktyā ।
ko hitaṁ mama tanoti vinā tvāṁ
dehi deśika mamāṅ‍ghryavalambam ॥ 8 ॥

śaṁkarāryakṛtasarvanibandha-
proktipāṭavamatīva vitīrya ।
aśrute'pi paribhāṣaṇadākṣyaṁ
dehi deśika mamāṅ‍ghryavalambam ॥ 9 ॥

vyāsajaiminikaṇādapadākṣaiḥ
pāṇiniprabhṛtibhirmunivaryaiḥ ।
proktaśāstraparipakvamanīṣāṁ
dehi deśika mamāṅ‍ghryavalambam ॥ 10 ॥

māramattagajacaṇḍamṛgendra
dārabandhumamatāgakuṭhāra ।
tārayāśu bhavavārinidhermāṁ
dehi deśika mamāṅ‍ghryavalambam ॥ 11 ॥

pādadhūli paripāvitaloka
bhedavādavanadāvakṛśāno ।
modamāna paramātmani nityaṁ
dehi deśika mamāṅ‍ghryavalambam ॥ 12 ॥

brāhmaṇatvamatidurlabhamāptaṁ
hyāśramaśca samavāpi turīyaḥ ।
nāsti rāgarahitatvamapīṣa-
ddehi deśika mamāṅ‍ghryavalambam ॥ 13 ॥
