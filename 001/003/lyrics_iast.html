॥ śrīśāradāgītam ॥

vidhimukhāmbhojahaṁsi śārade rakṣyāḥ kṛpayā

kamalākṣamukhyāmarapūjitapādāmbhoje ।
kamalāpradānadakṣe namatāṁ janānāṁ tvarayā ॥ 1 ॥

mukhadhūtacandrāmbhoje makhajāpatarpye budhaiḥ ।
sukhajñānasattārūpe sukhamāśu dattvānantam ॥ 2 ॥

kacajitanīlāmbhode kucadhūtacakre śive
acalena manasāpye racanāvanasvātantrye ॥ 3 ॥
