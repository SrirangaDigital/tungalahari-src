॥ śrīśāradāgītam ॥

śārade karuṇānidhe sakalānavāmba sadā janān ।
cāraṇādimagītavaibhavapūritākhiladiktate ॥ 1 ॥

bharmabhūṣaṇabhūṣite vararatnamaulivirājite ।
śarmadāyini karmamocini nirmalaṁ kuru mānasam ॥ 2 ॥

hastasandhṛtapustakākṣapaṭīsudhāghaṭamudrike ।
kastavāsti hi varṇane caturo naraḥ khacaro'thavā ॥ 3 ॥
