॥ śrī candramaulīśvara stutiḥ ॥

yasmiñjagatsarvamidaṁ vibhāti yaścāpyananyaḥ sakalaprapañcāt । tileṣu tailopamamāsthitaṁ tvāṁ śrīcandramaulīśvaramāśrayāmaḥ ॥ 1 ॥

aṇoraṇīyānmahato mahīyā - nātmā guhāyāṁ nihito'sya jantoḥ । tvāṁ sarvabhūtasthitamātmarūpaṁ śrīcandramaulīśvaramāśrayāmaḥ ॥ 2 ॥

yaḥ sarvasākṣī tamasaḥ parastā- dvibhāti sarvaṁ kila yasya bhāsā । tvāmāgamāntairapi durnirūpaṁ śrīcandramaulīśvaramāśrayāmaḥ ॥ 3 ॥

pumānavijñāya śivaṁ kadācid duḥkhasya nāntaṁ samupaiti dhīraḥ । tvāṁ saccidānandamayaṁ praśāntaṁ śrīcandramaulīśvaramāśrayāmaḥ ॥ 4 ॥

ajaṁ purāṇaṁ puruṣaṁ mahānta - manaśvaraṁ vāṅmanasātidūram । jñānātmanāṁ hṛdyavabhāsamānaṁ śrīcandramaulīśvaramāśrayāmaḥ ॥ 5 ॥

vadantyamī yatpadameva vedā jñāte tu yasmin sakalaṁ vibuddham । tvāṁ pratyagātmānamacintyaśaktiṁ śrīcandramaulīśvaramāśrayāmaḥ ॥ 6 ॥

yaṁ nirvikalpena samādhinaiva tapasvinaścetasi vītarāgāḥ । dhyāyanti tejomayamadbhutaṁ tvāṁ śrīcandramaulīśvaramāśrayāmaḥ ॥ 7 ॥

trilocanaṁ candrakalāvataṁsaṁ gaṅgātaraṅgollasaduttamāṅgam । smerāsyamiṣṭapradamaṣṭamūrtiṁ śrīcandramaulīśvaramāśrayāmaḥ ॥ 8 ॥

apārasaccitsukhavārirāśi - manādimadhyāntamanantarūpam । trilocanaṁ nīlagalaṁ praśāntaṁ śrīcandramaulīśvaramāśrayāmaḥ ॥ 9 ॥

kailāsaśailendraśirastaṭeṣu nagendraputryā saha saṁvasantam । vibhūtirudrākṣavibhūṣitāṅgaṁ śrīcandramaulīśvaramāśrayāmaḥ ॥ 10 ॥
