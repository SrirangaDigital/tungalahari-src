jaya deva jaya deva jaya sadgurunātha ।
śrībhāratītīrtha gurusārvabhauma ॥

varjitaviṣayakalāpa durjanasudurāpa ।
dūrīkṛtanatapāpa saccitsukharūpa ॥

pūjitapannagabhūṣaṇa prajñājitadhiṣaṇa ।
mukhanirdhutataruṇāruṇa bhakteṣu sakaruṇa ॥

vīkṣālavahṛtalokālaghuduḥsahaśoka ।
śaṅkaraguruvarapūjaka kṛtanatajanabhavuka ॥

paraśivaparāvatāra gatasarvavikāra ।
munijanahṛdayavihāra kṛtabhuvanoddhāra ॥
