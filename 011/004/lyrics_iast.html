॥ sadāśiveṁdra paṁcaratna stotram ॥

yatsandarśanamātrādbhaktirjātāpyaviddhakarṇasya ।
tatsandarśanamadhunā kṛtvā nūnaṁ kṛtārtho'smi ॥ 1 ॥

yo'niśamātmanyeva hyātmānaṁ sandadhadvīthyām ।
bhasmacchannānala iva jaḍākṛtiścarati taṁ naumi ॥ 2 ॥

yasya vilokanamātrāccetasi sañjāyate śīghram ।
vairāgyamacalamakhileṣvapi viṣayeṣu praṇaumi taṁ yaminam ॥ 3 ॥

purato bhavatu kṛpābdhiḥ puravairiniviṣṭamānasaḥ so'yam ।
paramaśivendrakarāmbujasañjāto yaḥ sadāśivendro me ॥ 4 ॥

unmattavatsañcaratīha śiṣyastaveti lokasya vacāṁsi śṛṇvan ।
khidyunnuvācāsya guruḥ purāho hyunmattatā me na hi tādṛśīti ॥ 5 ॥

pañcakametadbhaktyā ślokānāṁ viracitaṁ loke ।
yaḥ paṭhati so'pi labhate karuṇāṁ śīghraṁ sadāśivendrasya ॥ 6 ॥
